←   Whiskey

Description:

      Whiskey, also often spelled whisky, is a distilled alcoholic beverage made from fermented grain mash and is strictly regulated worldwide with many classes and types. Different types include American, Australian, Danish, English, Finnish, German, Canadian, Indian, Japanese, Irish, Swedish, and Welsh whiskey. Whiskey is always aged in wooden containers, usually made of white oak. The whiskeys produced in each country are mostly distinctive in character because of differences in the method of production, differences can include the type and character of cereal grains, and the quality and character of the water employed.
