←   Vermouth

Description:

      Vermouth is an aromatized, fortified wine which is flavored with various botanicals such as roots, barks, flowers, seeds, herbs, and spices. The name Vermouth derives from the German word Vermut, or “wormwood” As many as 40 different herbs and flavourings may be used in vermouth; the vermouths of various producers are often flavored according to closely guarded recipes. There are two main styles of vermouth: the French style, also known as the dry style, and the Italian, or sweet style, which is darker in color.
