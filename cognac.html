←   Cognac

Description:

      Cognac is an alcoholic beverage named after the French town of Cognac. Cognac is a brandy which by French law must be made from the wine of a specified grape variety. Cognac must follow strict production methods in order to meet legal requirements set by French law. These limitations control every step in production from the growing of the grapes to how the beverage is distilled.
