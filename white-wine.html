←   White Wine

Description:

      White wine is a type of wine whose color can vary between straw-yellow, yellow-green, yellow-gold. White wine is produced through the process of alcoholic fermentation. Unlike red wine, white wine is made from the non-colored pulp grapes which may have either white or black skin. The wine is treated in order to maintain the its transparent color in the final product.

      White wine has a reputation of being a more noble and impressive alcoholic beverage and is often associated with wealth in commercials. Despite its reputation, it is no less dangerous than other alcoholic beverages if an individual were to irresponsibly consume it.
